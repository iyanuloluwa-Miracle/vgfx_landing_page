How Vigilant works

Notice you've been defrauded? Open the vigilant app to report the incident, you will be required to fill the details of the transactions, make sure you fill it correctly and attach a file with your report, the file could be a screenshot of the receipt of the transactions, alert, etc. It important to upload or attach a file in order to support your claim of being defrauded.

Stage 2

You will then keep an eye on your reported incident, your incident is being monitored by the Nigeria Police Force and your affected bank, you can track the status, check comments and you can also comments as you notice relevant updates.

Stage 3

After your incident has been worked and reviewed on, you will be refunded by your bank into your bank account and if possible, who ever is responsible for the incident might be prosecuted.

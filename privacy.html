Privacy policy of vigilant App

Most importantly: we will not sell, trade, give, the information that we collect on our site to any third party, without your express permission. Vigilant app, is committed to safeguarding your privacy online. The following statement sets forth our privacy policy. For the purposes of this privacy statement ”Vigilant App” shall mean Vigilant App. This policy may change from time to time so please re-read this policy periodically.

What We Gather From You

Our site does not require user sensitive information. Our site collects information in several ways. When you register as a new user and lodge a financial complaint on our app you are required to fill a form. Vigilant App requests your name, phone, email address, financial details about complaints lodged and contact information (Customer Information). Your Bank Verification Number will also be requested to validate your identity. As part of our service, Vigilant App also uses cookies to help identify you and access your profile data, to track user behaviour, to track patterns and for system administration. A cookie is a small text file that is created by your browser from a web page and is stored on your computer’s hard drive.

Contact Information

Our site’s registration form requires users to give us contact information (like email address) and financial details about complaints lodged such as first name, last name, BVN, account number, transaction reference number, transaction receipt, amount lost and location from where the incident is being reported. Contact information is used to get in touch with them when necessary. Financial information collected is used to investigate complaints of customer fraud

Third Party Intermediaries

Vigilant App verifies the financial information given by you through 3rd party tracking entities and companies such as “Nigerian Police Force (NPF), Banks, Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN), Economic and Financial Crimes (EFCC) Commission and Nigeria Inter-Bank Settlement System Plc (NIBSS)”. These financial tracking entities and companies are solely a link in the tracking of financial fraud and do not store, retain or in any other way use the information they obtain from us.

Parties To Whom We Disclose Your Information

The data that we collect on your site will not be transmitted to a 3rd party without your express permission. Meaning, that we will not, and do not, mine your data for marketing purposes, nor disclose the details of your traffic to a third party, without your express permission. Vigilant App may disclose Customer Information in special cases where Vigilant App has reason to believe that disclosing this information is necessary to identity, contact or bring legal action against someone who may be violating our terms and conditions of use or may be causing injury or interference with our rights, property, our customers or anyone who could be harmed by such activities. We may also disclose Customer Information when we believe, in good faith, that the law requires it and for administrative and other purposes necessary to maintain services and improve our products and services. You should also note that the servers hosting our systems are located in a number of different countries including Nigeria. This means that the information you provide will be transferred to and hosted on servers in these countries and by submitting the requested information you hereby expressly consent to such transfers.

Security

This site has security measures in place to protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. A password must be entered to log in to your account (profile) and in order to manage your profile data. All personal information is fully encrypted in transit during all transactions. Please note, however, that no data transmission over the internet is completely secure. As a result, while we endeavor to protect your personal information, we cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you provide to us and you transmit such information to us at your own risk. Once we receive your transmission we endeavour to ensure the security of your personal information on our systems. Please note that Vigilant App will never ask for any personal information via email that includes information such as marriage status, income, etc.
